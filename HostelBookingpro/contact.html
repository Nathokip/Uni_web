Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hostel?

Simply browse available hostels, select your preferred dates, and click "Book Now." You'll need to create an account and complete the booking process.

Are the hostels verified?

Yes! All hostels and landlords undergo thorough verification including document checks, property inspections, and background verification.

What's the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before move-in. After that, cancellation fees may apply based on the specific hostel's policy.

How do I become a landlord?

Visit our "For Landlords" page to start the verification process. You'll need to submit required documents and complete the registration.
